Packs the Button widget into a specific position within its parent frame.

Args:
    - side (str): The side of the frame to place the widget. 
        Possible values are "TOP", "LEFT", "RIGHT", or "BOTTOM".
    - padx (int | tuple, optional): Horizontal padding around the widget. 
    - pady (int | tuple, optional): Vertical padding around the widget. 
    - anchor (str, optional): Alignment of the widget relative to the packing side.
        This parameter is not currently used in the method.
